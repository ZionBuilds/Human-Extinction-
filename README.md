

# 🧠 Human Extinction

A fast-paced arcade survival game built with **Python + Pygame**, featuring escalating waves, adaptive enemy AI, boss phases, and a chaotic visual “juice” system inspired by arcade brutality and stylized neon violence.

---

## ⚡ Overview

**Human Extinction** is a wave-based survival game where the player (Carmon) fights against evolving human enemies and increasingly powerful boss entities.

The deeper you go, the more unstable the world becomes.

Waves scale endlessly until the final boss is triggered at wave 30.

---

Trigger escalating wave difficulty

---

👁️ Player: Carmon

Carmon is not a traditional hero.

She is:

A pulsing entity of motion and force

Emotionally unreadable (no eyes, only presence)

Driven by instinct-like movement

Connected to destruction-based progression


💗 Carmon Design Philosophy

No facial expression

Only a heartbeat pulse aura

Movement is fluid and reactive

Represents “living pressure” rather than a person


Carmon is less of a character and more of a force the world reacts to.


---

🧍 Humans (Enemies)

Humans are adaptive AI entities with 3 behaviors:

🔴 Rusher

Directly charges player

High aggression


🔵 Orbiter

Circles player dynamically

Maintains pressure zone


🟢 Shooter

Retreats and avoids close range

Reacts defensively when near player


👁️ Visual Identity

Each human includes:

Simple body shape

Dual glowing eyes

Subtle motion pulse


They represent reactive survival instinct.


---

👑 Boss System

At Wave 30, the Final Boss awakens.

🧬 Boss Phases

Phase 1: Direct pursuit

Phase 2: Erratic movement bursts

Phase 3: Aggressive acceleration


💀 Boss Behavior

Tracks player position

Adapts movement based on HP

Becomes faster and more unstable over time



---

💥 FX / Juice System

The game uses heavy visual feedback:

✨ Effects

Screen shake on impact

Rainbow distortion flashes

Slash particle bursts

Blood splatter decals

Motion trail afterimages


🩸 Blood System

Enemy death leaves permanent splatter marks

Creates evolving battlefield visuals



---

🌈 Visual Style

The game uses a neon-chaos aesthetic:

Dark backgrounds

Hot pink / red combat palette

Glowing overlays during boss fights

Subtle “shader-like” lighting feel


The goal is controlled visual chaos.

🧠 Game Identity / Lore

In the world of Human Extinction:

Carmon is the triggering event of escalation.

Every action she takes causes the world to respond:

More enemies spawn

Difficulty increases

Reality destabilizes at higher waves

The Final Boss emerges as a system response


💔 Core Theme

> “The world does not fight Carmon — it adapts to her existence.”
